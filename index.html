Our Home ground

Pruntrutermatte

Basel RFC is proud to call Pruntrutermatte our home. Located at the heart of Basel, our club is a state-of-the-art facility that hosts matches for all our teams throughout the season.

Address: Pruntrutermatte, Basel, Switzerland

Capacity: Full-sized rugby pitch with modern facilities

Features: Well-maintained grass rugby field, club house, running track.
