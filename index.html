St. Jakob Stadium

Basel RFC is proud to call St. Jakob Stadium our home. Located at the heart of Basel, our stadium is a state-of-the-art facility that hosts matches for all our teams throughout the season.

Address: St. Jakob Park, Basel, Switzerland

Capacity: Full-sized rugby pitch with modern facilities

Features: Professional-grade rugby field, training grounds, spectator seating, and comprehensive amenities.
